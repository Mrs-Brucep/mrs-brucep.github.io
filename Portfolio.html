Welcome to the Village

Our strong and robust village is best known for our plethora of previous hokage who were all considered the best of their time. Hashirama Senju of the Senju clan is the most renowned kage of all time considered to be a god of shinobi.

Our Village History

The Hidden Leaf Village is the first village to have been established. During the Warring States period the two most predominant clans the Senju and Uchiha clans often clashed. But Hashirama Senju and Madara Uchiha mended the animosity between the clans and created this scenic village decorated with leaves.
